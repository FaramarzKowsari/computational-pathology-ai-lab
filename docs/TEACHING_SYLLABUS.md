# Teaching Syllabus

A 12-session graduate or professional module can be taught directly from the repository:

1. Digital pathology and problem formulation
2. Data provenance, labels, and leakage
3. CNN baseline engineering
4. Transfer learning and controlled comparisons
5. Vision Transformers and self-supervised representations
6. Foundation-model adapter design and licensing
7. WSI processing and patch extraction
8. Multiple Instance Learning
9. Medical-AI metrics and calibration
10. Explainability, uncertainty, and domain shift
11. MLOps, APIs, containers, and monitoring
12. Reproducible reporting, governance, open-source release, and DOI publication

Every session should distinguish executable evidence from planned research.
