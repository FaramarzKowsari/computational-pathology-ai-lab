# Research Questions

1. How does performance change at 1%, 5%, 10%, 25%, and 100% label budgets?
2. Do frozen pathology encoders improve calibration as well as discrimination?
3. Which architectures are most robust to stain, blur, compression, and brightness shifts?
4. How stable are explanations across seeds and model families?
5. Can uncertainty identify error-enriched cases without hiding subgroup failures?
6. How much does patient-level splitting reduce optimistic bias relative to patch-level splitting?
7. Does attention MIL improve slide-level evidence over mean pooling?
8. What is the accuracy-latency-memory tradeoff for CPU and GPU deployment?

Each question must be answered with executed artifacts; planned results remain `Not Yet Benchmarked`.
